WHO WE ARE

ABOUT HUGO

Hugo is an 18-year-old young creator with a big dream and an even bigger passion. He is the founder of “Popscast,” a podcast he built from the ground up with the hope of reaching listeners all around the world. Every episode he creates reflects his enthusiasm, creativity, and desire to connect with people through meaningful and entertaining content. Hugo’s dream is to become globally known so that everyone, no matter where they are, can hear about Popscast and join him on this journey. With determination and talent, he is working every day to turn that dream into reality.

ABOUT GABI

Gabi, a 21-year-old League of Legends proplayer, already had fame in eSports. However, his childhood friendship with Hugo took him to the next level. His appearance on Hugo's podcast was a massive boost. The show not only introduced Gabi to a wider audience but also allowed him to showcase his charisma and share personal anecdotes. This leap turned him into a full digital personality, expanding his influence far beyond the Summoner's Rift and solidifying his media success.
